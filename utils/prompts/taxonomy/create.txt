Create: Put elements together to form a coherent or functional whole; reorganize elements into a new pattern or structure.

- Generating: Coming up with alternative hypotheses based on criteria (e.g., Generate hypotheses to account for an observed phenomenon)
- Planning: Devising a procedure for accomplishing some task (e.g., Plan a research paper on a given historical topic)
- Producing: Inventing a product (e.g., Build habitats for a specific purpose)
